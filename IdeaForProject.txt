Idea for the online-retailer website, part of it is based on Duke List

Administrator:
1. Delete a product
2. Delete a user
3. Modify a product
4. Modify user information
5. check purchases based on user
6. check purchases based on seller

Seller:
1. Post a product
2. delete a product
3. modify seller info
4. confirm sending
5. check balance
6. register

Buyer:
1. Save a product to wish list
2. Buy a product
3. Follow/unfollow a seller
4. Leave a comment
5. modify buyer info
6. Search for a product
7. check sold product
8. rate a product
9. register
10. check purchases
11. report not receiving
